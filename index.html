Our Vision

New League is a team of seasoned entrepreneurs dedicated to your success. Our goal is to disrupt the incubator and accelerator system because it is broken. Here is why: Too many amazing founders get turned away from or have no access to the few elite groups that hold court over the startup world. Or worse, go through a program only to fail because they weren’t given the hands on help and resources they really need.

We said enough! And created what we believe is a better solution, one that immediately provides for a much higher startup success rate and more support for founders than has ever been offered before.

How It Works

At New League, we don’t believe in a cookie-cutter approach to helping our founder teams. Instead, we have created two programs that you can customize to fit your needs.

5 Step Startup Program

Our 5 Step Startup Program is a three month program for seed and early stage startups that need funding, company formation and management, product and market strategy and product design and development.

Evaluation

From our very first meeting you will realize how different we are. We talk, you pitch, then together we discuss in great detail, your ideas and plans for your business. We will quickly analyze and validate your direction or provide additional concepts, models or strategies for you to consider.

Mentor Team & Resource Allocation

Once we agree to move forward, we will work closely with your team to identify and match you with the best mentors and offer the right resources to develop your MVP, product or service. Our mentors provide high-level strategy and guidance as well as direct hands-on development as required.

Business Structure & Foundation

While developing your product or service is an obvious priority, your company must also be properly formed and prepared to handle the myriad of issues and opportunities that will present themselves. With New League you have a full legal team at your disposal to help you with company formation, equity & dilution, provide you with all the necessary business agreement forms etc.

Minimal Viable Product Development

You’ll have 3 months to develop your MVP. If you already have an MVP we will develop a custom schedule for your team to meet your goals. We will also help get you ready for Demo Day.

Demo Day

On Demo Day you will demonstrate your product and pitch your business model strategy to a group of pre-qualified investors that are focused on your technology category.

Talent Cloud

Our second program, we call Talent Cloud. This is our consulting services program for startups with gaps in expertise or have special needs. Go to the Talent Cloud to find pre-qualified consultants in virtually every aspect of business, product design or technology development to temporarily or permanently augment your team.

Need an additional engineer or even a team of twenty? Done. Wish you had a hot UX designer? Done. Need legal representation to help navigate founder equity and the tax code? Done. This level of support is unheard of in this business!

The Talent Cloud is completely ala carte and becomes your one-stop-shop, saving your team time and the anxiety of locating and qualifying additional resources.
